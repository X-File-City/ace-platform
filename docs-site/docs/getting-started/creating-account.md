# Creating an Account

Get started with ACE by creating and verifying your account.

## Sign Up

### Option 1: Email & Password

1. Visit [app.aceagent.io](https://app.aceagent.io)
2. Click **Sign Up**
3. Enter your email address
4. Create a strong password (minimum 8 characters)
5. Click **Create Account**
6. Check your email for verification link

### Option 2: OAuth Providers

Sign up instantly using:

- **Google** - Use your Google Workspace or personal account
- **GitHub** - Recommended for developers

OAuth signup automatically verifies your email.

## Email Verification

Email verification is required to:

- Create API keys
- Record outcomes
- Trigger evolutions
- Access billing features

### Verification Process

1. Check your inbox for an email from `noreply@aceagent.io`
2. Click the **Verify Email** button
3. You'll be redirected to the dashboard
4. A confirmation banner shows verification succeeded

### Didn't Receive the Email?

- Check your spam/junk folder
- Wait a few minutes and try again
- Click **Resend Verification** in the dashboard
- Contact support if issues persist

## Account Settings

After verification, configure your account:

### Profile

- **Display Name** - How you appear in the dashboard
- **Email** - Your login email (can be changed)
- **Avatar** - Pulled from OAuth provider or Gravatar

### Security

- **Change Password** - Update your password
- **Connected Accounts** - Link additional OAuth providers
- **Sessions** - View and revoke active sessions

### Notifications

Configure email notifications for:

- Evolution completions
- Usage alerts
- Security notifications
- Product updates

## Data & Privacy

### Your Data

- Playbooks are private to your account
- Outcomes are encrypted at rest
- We never train on your data

### Data Export

Export your data anytime:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Privacy**
2. Click **Export My Data**
3. Download includes playbooks, outcomes, and settings

### Account Deletion

To delete your account:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Privacy**
2. Click **Delete Account**
3. Confirm by typing your email
4. All data is permanently deleted within 30 days

:::warning
Account deletion is irreversible. Export your data first if needed.
:::

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In

- **Forgot password?** Use the password reset flow
- **OAuth not working?** Ensure third-party cookies are enabled
- **Account locked?** Contact support after 5 failed attempts

### Verification Email Not Arriving

1. Check spam folder
2. Add `noreply@aceagent.io` to contacts
3. Try a different email address
4. Contact support with your signup email

### OAuth Provider Issues

- Ensure you're using a supported browser
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try incognito/private mode
- Check provider status pages
